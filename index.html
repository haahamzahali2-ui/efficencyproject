Helping Hands Referral System
Gynecologist
  1. Ensure it’s not a preventive women’s health visit. Look for “pelvic pain, abdominal pain, chronic pain,” etc., on Athena.
  2. Print referral order and place in the bin.
  3. Click the link below and enter patient information:
    4. Enter Patient Info
  4. Columbus Free Clinic will call the patient to confirm the appointment.
  5. Tell patient to only go to the clinic once they receive a call or voicemail.
Women’s Health
  1. Ensure it’s a preventative visit, not a gynecology referral.
  2. Grab the women’s health scheduling clipboard from the front.
  3. Go to patient room, ask what time works best, and put their name down.
  4. Go to Athena and schedule the patient at the chosen time.
Neurologist
  1. Neurology visits are done here — check with leadership for neurologist availability.
  2. Once availability is confirmed, schedule the patient to come back.
  3. Make sure to assign the patient to the appropriate provider.
Cardiologist
  1. Print referral and place in the bin.
  2. Tell patients they will receive a phone call from PhysicianCareConnection to schedule an appointment.
Mammograms

Done at OSU:

  1. Print order and place in bin.
  2. Ask Trena and she will take care of it.
Physical Therapy
  1. Print referral and place in bin.
  2. Fill out the form below:
    3. Fill Out Form
  3. Instruct patients to wait for a phone call to confirm the schedule, and to not go to the clinic without confirmation.
EKG
  1. Do NOT print.
  2. These are done in our clinic, take patient to lab.
Optometry
  1. Instruct patient to go to Dwell community clinic located at (1934 N. 4th Street, 43201).
  2. Only open on 1st and 3rd Monday of every month from 6PM to 7PM.
Dental (Emergency)
  1. Print referral order and place in bin.
  2. Instruct patients that Grace Free Clinic will call them to schedule an appointment.
