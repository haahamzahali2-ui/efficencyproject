Helping Hands Referral System

Internal referral guide for clinic volunteers and staff

Important – Please read before doing any referral
  • Referrals no longer need to be printed. They will now be shared on Athena.
  • Please enable information sharing on Athena before doing any referral.
  • This shares the patient's Athena profile with the referred provider.
Gynecologist
  1. Ensure it is NOT a preventive women’s health visit.
  2. Print referral order and place in bin.
  3. Enter patient information in the form below.
    4. Open Referral Form
  4. Columbus Free Clinic will call the patient.
Women's Health
  1. Confirm this is a preventative visit.
  2. Grab women's health scheduling clipboard.
  3. Ask preferred time and record Athena ID, DOB, phone.
  4. Give patient a reminder card.
  5. Schedule the visit on Athena.
Neurologist
  1. Visits are usually once per month on Thursday.
  2. Confirm availability with leadership.
  3. Schedule patient on Athena with Dr Leon Rosenburg.
Cardiologist
  1. Print referral and place in bin.
  2. Patient will receive call from PhysicianCareConnection.
  3. This is the only free cardiology option.
Mammograms
  1. Print order and place in bin.
  2. Ask Trena to process the referral.
Physical Therapy
  1. Print referral and place in bin.
  2. Fill out form below.
    3. Fill Out Form
  3. Patient must wait for confirmation phone call.
EKG
  1. Do NOT print referral.
  2. Take patient directly to lab.
Optometry
  1. Send patient to Dwell Community Clinic.
  2. Address: 1934 N 4th St.
  3. Open 1st and 3rd Monday each month 6-7 PM.
Dental (Emergency)
  1. Print referral order.
  2. Grace Free Clinic will call patient.
Dental (Non Emergency)
  1. Print referral order.
  2. Grace Free Clinic will call patient.
Diabetic Retinopathy
  1. Print referral order.
  2. Enter patient info in form below.
    3. Enter Patient Info
  3. Clinic will call patient.
Ultrasound
  1. Print referral order.
  2. Enter patient info in form below.
    3. Enter Patient Info
  3. Clinic will call patient to confirm.
Physicals for Work / School
  1. Done at Grace Free Clinic.
  2. Send patient to the locations below.
    3. View Locations
